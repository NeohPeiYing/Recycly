Robotic Disassembly Machines

Breaking down products for smarter recycling

Robotic disassembly machines are designed to take apart complex products such as electronics, appliances, and vehicles. Using advanced tools and computer vision, these robots identify parts, unscrew components, and safely remove hazardous materials.

For example, when disassembling an old smartphone, the robot can separate the battery, circuit boards, and casing into different containers so each material can be recycled or disposed of safely.

This automation reduces the need for manual labor in dangerous environments, improves efficiency, and ensures valuable resources are recovered with minimal waste.

As technology evolves, these machines could handle more product types, making large-scale, safe, and precise recycling possible worldwide.
